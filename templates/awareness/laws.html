🚫 Anti-Ragging Laws & Guidelines

Know your rights and responsibilities — stay safe, respectful, and ragging-free!

📜 UGC Guidelines

The UGC mandates strict action against ragging — suspension, expulsion, or rustication.

⚖️ Legal Action

Ragging is a punishable crime under IPC Sections 323, 341, 506. Offenders face jail time.

🏫 Campus Rules

Every college must form an Anti-Ragging Committee and provide helpline numbers to students.

“Respect is Strength. Ragging is Weakness.”

🛡️ What To Do If You Face Ragging?

  • 📞 Call the 24x7 National Anti-Ragging Helpline: 1800-180-5522
  • ✉️ Email your complaint to helpline@antiragging.in
  • 🏫 Report immediately to your college Anti-Ragging Committee
  • ⚖️ If ignored, file a police complaint under IPC laws.
